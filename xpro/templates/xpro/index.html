About TFN

Teach For Nigeria (TFN) is a Non-profit organization focused on developing a movement of leaders across the nation who are committed to ending educational inequity. Through a two-year Fellowship, TFN recruits Nigeria’s best and brightest graduates and young professionals of all academic disciplines to teach as full-time teachers (Fellows) in underserved schools in disadvantaged communities. Through this experience, our Fellows gain exposure to the realities of Nigeria’s education system and begin to identify their role in building a wider movement for educational equity. At Teach For Nigeria, we believe that there is no single solution to solving the education crisis in the country and as a result of this we encourage our second-year Fellows to work on various projects (called Be The Change Projects) proffering many solutions to challenges associated with delivering access to quality education to children in their placement schools/communities. So far, our Fellows have implemented a total of 72 Be The Change projects. In the long-term, we build and support a powerful force of Alumni, who, informed by their experiences and insights, work towards affecting systemic changes to realize educational opportunity for all. Our Alumni work in diverse roles within the education sector - as teachers, teacher-trainers, school curriculum designers, education researchers, social entrepreneurs and non profit leaders.
